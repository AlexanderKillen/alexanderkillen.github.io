Self-designed Linear Microphone Array

Designed the PCB in KiCad [View Whole Project]. Corrected wiring errors by manually re-routing 0.2 mm traces [View Closeup of Re-soldering] using a razor blade [View Cut Traces] and fine-gauge copper wire.

The system utilises an STM32 as an intermediary driver to sample sound from 8 PDM-mics, convert to PCM, and package the data for transmission over USB-OTG (with CRC) to Python.
